- king tags obstacles: these become sources of income
- engineer can touch an obstacle and construct a tower (this costs money). Towers will lose range over time and eventually just melt
- engineer can also touch an existing friendly tower and extend its radius and life (this costs money)
- engineer can also touch an enemy tower and tear it down a bit (this costs money).
- general can spend money to spawn creeps.
- Creeps chase kings and take down their HP (0 HP = gg). Creeps also slowly melt.
- Towers shoot down creeps
- Towers repel generals
